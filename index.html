About

Hey, I’m Liam.

I’m a North Bay photographer drawn to the moments that feel real — kids running around, couples laughing, families just being themselves. My goal is to capture those small, honest moments in a way that feels natural, warm, and a little timeless. I keep sessions relaxed and simple so nothing feels forced. A bit of guidance when it helps, plenty of space for things to unfold naturally — and photos that reflect how the moment actually felt.

  • Natural light and warm tones
  • Relaxed sessions with gentle guidance
  • Clean, thoughtfully edited images

What I shoot

Couples, families, and portraits — laid-back sessions and real moments.

  • Family sessions + events
  • Graduation + portraits
